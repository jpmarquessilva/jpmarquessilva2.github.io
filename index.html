About me

I am a Research Chair at ANITI, University of Toulouse. My research interests include automated reasoning, artificial intelligence and machine learning. Before joining ANITI, I was affiliated with University College Dublin, the University of Southampton, and with the University of Lisbon. At ANITI, I am the Research Chair on Deep Learner Verification and Optimization.
